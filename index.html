The Arcade Cabinet Model

This model was built in Blender, with textures done on Photoshop and Procreate. The plan was to build the games in Unity, and then to render them onto the screen of the cabinet. While we completed the games and cabinet model, putting them all together into one executable as we originally envisioned proved to be a challenge. You can view the model here, and click on the links below to play the individual games.
